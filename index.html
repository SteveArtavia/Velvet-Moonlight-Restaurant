Indulge in Our Culinary Creations

At Velvet Moonlight, each dish is a celebration of flavor and artistry. Discover some of our signature dishes that exemplify our commitment to excellence and creativity in every bite.

Truffle-Infused Filet Mignon

Tender filet mignon, perfectly seared and enhanced with a rich truffle sauce. Accompanied by roasted garlic mashed potatoes and seasonal vegetables, this dish promises a luxurious dining experience.

Lobster Ravioli

Delicate ravioli stuffed with fresh lobster meat, served in a creamy saffron sauce with a hint of lemon zest. Garnished with microgreens, this dish offers a sophisticated twist on classic Italian cuisine.

Chocolate Fondant

Decadent molten chocolate cake with a gooey center, paired with a scoop of homemade vanilla bean ice cream. This dessert is the perfect sweet ending to your elegant meal.

Rosemary & Garlic Roasted Chicken

Succulent chicken roasted with rosemary and garlic, served with a side of truffle-infused risotto and grilled asparagus. This hearty dish combines comfort with gourmet flair.
